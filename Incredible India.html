Dharamshala

Little tibet in india.

A pristine green expanse ringed by the mist-shrouded Himalayas and a dense cover of oak, deodar, pine and conifers, Dharamsala (Dharamshala), in Himachal Pradesh, is a secluded natural retreat.
Triund Trek

A popular hill station in the Dharamkot area of McLeodganj.

Throughout the trek, views of the entire Dhauladhar range and the vast, lush green meadows offer a surreal experience that attracts several trekkers looking for a moderate-to-easy trek for the weekend.
Shillong

Explore Shillong city and its best places to visit.

Colonial-era charm, a landscape of rolling green and pristine mountains come together to bring to life the city of Shillong, the capital of Meghalaya. Its vistas are accentuated by pine trees, streams, waterfalls and lush green parks.
