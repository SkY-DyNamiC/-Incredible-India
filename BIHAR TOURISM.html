SPECIAL SWEETS OF BIHAR

Bihar has a variety of special sweet dish which comes with a good hygiene as well as with a Great taste. From all over the world people comes to bihar to explore the culture of bihar as a gift we serve them our lovings from this delecious sweets.

LAUNG LATA

Famous Bihari regional sweet laung Latta has many names like lavang lata, laung Latika, etc. Laung Lata is crispy on the outer surface and a little juicy inside. The outer layer is made using all-purpose flour and it is filled with a mix of milk mava and nuts. The outer layer is sealed using lavang (clove) which gives an exotic aroma to it.

BUNDI LADOO

Laddu, or ladoo, are popular Indian treats often enjoyed during holidays and festivals, or just about any time there is an occasion to celebrate. They are round ball-shaped sweet snacks that are made with all kinds of ingredients.

PERUKIYA

Chhapra town of Bihar is particularly famous for Perukia. It is prepared by mixing Sooji (semolina) or khoa with sugar and wrapping the mixture in thin leaves made from a mix of maida, water and ghee. The sweet is then deep fried in ghee and served.

THEKUA

Thekua is a traditional deep-fried sweet snack from Bihar made from whole wheat flour (atta). Thekua is also called kajuria, khajoor, and many more names. The key thekua ingredients are whole wheat flour (atta), ghee, and jaggery (gur or unrefined brown sugar). it is traditionally made with jaggery, which makes it healthy and tasty.
